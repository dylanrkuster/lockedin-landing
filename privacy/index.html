Legal

Privacy Policy

Your data stays on your device. No accounts. No data collection from app usage.

Data We Access

LOCKEDIN requires access to:

  • Apple Health: We read workout duration and step count to calculate earned screen time.
  • Screen Time: Required to block selected apps at the system level via Apple FamilyControls.

Data We Collect

The LOCKEDIN app does not collect, transmit, or store your personal health data, screen time data, or Apple Health data on external servers. All of that stays on your device.

  • No accounts required
  • No advertising
  • No selling of user data

Analytics and Diagnostics

LOCKEDIN uses Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to capture anonymous usage events (e.g. onboarding completion, paywall views, subscription events) and crash reports. These help us improve stability and product quality. No personal health data, screen time data, or identifying information is sent to these services.

Subscriptions

Subscription purchases are processed by Apple via StoreKit. LOCKEDIN does not see or store your payment information. Apple's privacy practices for in-app purchases apply — see apple.com/legal/privacy.

Website Analytics

This website (getlockedin.dev) uses Google Analytics to understand how visitors find LOCKEDIN. This collects anonymous, aggregate page-visit data only. It is entirely separate from the app.

Data Storage

All app data — your screen time balance, blocked apps list, workout history, and settings — is stored locally on your device and in the iOS app group container. It never leaves your device except for the anonymous analytics events described above.

Children

LOCKEDIN is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we change this policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date below. Material changes will be surfaced in the app.
