Questions

Good to know

Does it actually block my apps?

Yes — at the system level, using Apple's Screen Time framework. It's real blocking, not a reminder you can swipe away. To open a locked app you spend XP for a timed window.

What counts as movement?

Any steps or exercise minutes Apple Health tracks. It works with Apple Watch and anything that syncs to Health — Garmin, Whoop, Oura, Fitbit. No camera, no propping your phone up.

How do ranks work?

You climb a fixed ladder of 24 ranks — eight metals (Bronze through Obsidian), three tiers each. Moving always earns the same XP; what changes is that unlocking gets more expensive as you climb, so higher ranks ask for more movement and less scrolling.

Is it free?

LOCKEDIN is a paid app with a 7-day free trial. After the trial it's $6.99/week or $39.99/year. Cancel anytime in Settings.

What about my privacy?

We only keep what's needed to run your plan and sync your progress across a reinstall. Our full privacy policy spells out exactly what that is.
