Legal

Privacy Policy

LOCKEDIN is built with privacy as a core principle. Your data stays on your device. No accounts. No tracking. No BS.

Data We Access

LOCKEDIN requires access to the following on your device:

  • Apple Health (HealthKit): We read workout duration and type to calculate earned screen time. We do not access heart rate, weight, or any other health metrics.
  • Screen Time (FamilyControls): Required to block selected apps. This is the core functionality.

Data We Collect

None. Zero. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your data on external servers.

  • No accounts required
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertising
  • No data sharing with third parties

Data Storage

All app data (balance, history, blocked apps, settings) is stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device.

Data Retention

Transaction history is retained for 7 days, then automatically deleted. Uninstalling removes all data.

Third-Party Services

LOCKEDIN does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. The only integrations are Apple's native frameworks (HealthKit and FamilyControls).

Children's Privacy

LOCKEDIN is not designed for children under 13. The app requires user control over device Screen Time settings.
