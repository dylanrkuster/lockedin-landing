The Difference

Screen time limits don't work because you can ignore them.

You've tried the built-in limits. You've tried "mindful" apps. You tap through them without thinking. They fail because there's no real consequence.

LOCKEDIN is different.
When your balance hits zero, your apps are blocked at the system level. No snooze. No workarounds. The only way back in is to move your body.

Movement is the only password.

How It Works

Simple as 1-2-3

01

Pick apps to block

Select the apps that waste your time. Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, games. You decide.

02

Move to earn

Workouts and steps both count. Any exercise tracked in Apple Health earns you screen time.

03

Use apps, spend balance

Every minute on blocked apps costs a minute from your balance. Hit zero and you're locked out.

Earning

Workouts and steps both count

You don't need to hit the gym. Any movement tracked by Apple Health earns screen time. Works with Apple Watch, Garmin, Whoop, Oura, Fitbit, and any device that syncs to Apple Health.

Workouts

Run, lift, cycle, swim. Any workout synced to Apple Health.

Steps

Every 1,000 steps earns screen time. Just walk.
