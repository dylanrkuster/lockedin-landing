Questions

Good to know

Does it actually block my apps?

Yes — at the system level, using Apple's Screen Time framework. It's real blocking, not a reminder you can swipe away. To open a locked app you spend XP for a timed session.

What counts as movement?

Any steps or exercise minutes Apple Health tracks. It works with Apple Watch and anything that syncs to Health — Garmin, Whoop, Oura, Fitbit. No camera, no propping your phone up.

How do the ranks work?

You climb a fixed ladder of eight ranks — Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, Emerald, Sapphire, Diamond, Obsidian. Moving always earns the same XP; what changes is that unlocking gets more expensive as you climb, so higher ranks ask for more movement and less scrolling.

What is "life won back"?

An honest, conservative estimate of the time you're reclaiming, based on published research linking extra daily movement to a longer life. Only movement above your usual day counts, and the credit eases off the more you do — it's an estimate, not a medical promise.

Is it free?

What about my privacy?

We only keep what's needed to run your plan and sync your progress across a reinstall. Our full privacy policy spells out exactly what that is.
