The Psychology: Why Exercise-Gating Works

Opal's time-based approach has a fundamental flaw: you can always end the session early. When a craving hits, you have the power to override it. And you will.

LOCKEDIN flips the script. There's no "end session" button. The only way to unlock your apps is to physically move your body. This works because:

  1. Exercise replaces the dopamine. You're not just removing the bad habit — you're substituting it with something that produces real, lasting neurochemicals.
  2. The cost is physical, not mental. Willpower is finite. Exercise is concrete. "Do 20 pushups" is easier to follow than "resist checking Instagram for 4 more hours."
  3. It builds a positive feedback loop. Less screen time + more exercise = feeling better = wanting to continue.
